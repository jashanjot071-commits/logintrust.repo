FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Login Trust.

Is Login Trust free to use?

Yes — creating an account and running scans is completely free. No credit card required.

What types of URLs can I scan?

Any public http or https URL. We block internal/private IP addresses for security reasons. Paste the full URL including the protocol (https://...).

How accurate is the scanner?

Our scanner uses multiple heuristic checks including URL structure analysis, domain reputation signals, and known phishing patterns to deliver high-confidence verdicts.

Is my scan data stored or shared?

We do not sell or share your scan data with third parties. Scans are processed to generate results and are not linked to personal profiles.
